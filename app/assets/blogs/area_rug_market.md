## Market Restraints

### Key Challenges
1. **High Raw Material Costs**
   - Accounts for ~28% of production and installation costs
   - ~18 million units faced affordability challenges in 2025

2. **Maintenance Requirements**
   - Limits adoption in some residential and commercial segments
   - Particularly impacts North America and Europe markets

3. **Competition from Hard Flooring**
   - Alternative floor coverings pose ongoing competition
   - Expected to have limited long-term impact

## India-US Trade Dynamics

### India Market Position
- **Second-largest exporter** of carpets and rugs globally (after China)
- **Largest producer** of handmade carpets by volume and value
- 90% of production exported internationally
- Over 2 million workers employed in carpet industry
- Exports to 70+ countries worldwide

### India Export Data
| Period | Export Value | Growth |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| **2021-22** | USD 2.23 billion | +19.5% |
| **Handmade carpets 2021-22** | USD 1.51 billion | +18.8% |
| **April-Sept 2022** | USD 710 million | - |

### USA as Import Destination
- **Largest importer** of Indian carpets and floor coverings
- 57% of India's carpet exports go to USA
- **2021-22 imports from India**: USD 1.28 billion (up from USD 1.06 billion previous year)
- Primary imports: Handmade carpets (majority), machine-made, tufted

### US Carpet Industry
- The carpet industry supports over **250,000 direct jobs**
- Over **1 million indirect jobs** in the USA
- Major production hub: Dalton, Georgia region
